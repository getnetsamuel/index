Ramada by Wyndham Addis - Best Hotel in Addis Ababa

Located in the heart of the city and just five minutes away from the airport, Ramada by Wyndham Addis, Addis Ababa, offers a supremely comfortable and relaxing experience. Conveniently close to major corporate and government offices like the African Union headquarters, as well as tourist attractions like the National Museum, it’s the perfect choice for discerning tourists. Our 134 guest rooms are equipped with plush amenities to ensure a memorable stay. Ramada Hotel offers two distinct dining venues: Chefs Club, where guests can enjoy a diverse array of dishes starting with a hearty breakfast and continuing with a Mediterranean a la carte menu for lunch and dinner, complemented by local specialties. The hotel's rooftop bar and lounge, Fogo no Chao, presents a Brazilian steakhouse experience from 5:30 PM to 10:30 PM, featuring beef, chicken, pork, and lamb. Sundays feature an international brunch from 12:00 PM to 3:00 PM, showcasing a blend of local and global cuisine against stunning city vistas. Additionally, the Connect Lobby Bar at our hotel near Addis Ababa Airport serves quick bites and sandwiches in a relaxed setting from noon until 10:30 PM.
